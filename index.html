CONTROLS

Artillery: higth rate of fire and DMG.

Laser: laser beams pass through enemies.

You can upgrade artillery to laser guns.

You can upgrade the damage of any turret.

Build silos to gain more money. Tip: highly recommended ;)

Use to choose the position you want to buid in and Space Bar to build or Esc to cancel.

Use the right bar to build:

Enjoy!
